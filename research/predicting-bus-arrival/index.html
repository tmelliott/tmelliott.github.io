Predicting bus arrival

August 22, 2020

For half a decade, I explored ways of predicting bus arrival with improved reliability. I came up with a framework that uses the GTFS information to construct a transit network, allowing buses to share information about traffic conditions, even between routes.

library(magrittr)

I then implement a particle filter to estimate vehicle state and ultimately road speeds which are passed to the network model, a Kalman filter running in real-time estimating current traffic conditions. From there, using a particle filter, it's fairly straightforward to estimate arrival time distributions.
